# User Stories

## Epic 1: Website Creation

**As a** non-technical founder,
**I want** to create a website without writing any code,
**So that** I can launch my idea quickly.

- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - The platform should provide a drag-and-drop interface.
  - The platform should offer pre-designed templates.
  - The platform should allow customization of templates.
  - The platform should support basic SEO settings.
  - The platform should allow for easy publishing.
- **Complexity**: M

**As a** non-technical founder,
**I want** to choose from a variety of templates,
**So that** I can find a design that fits my brand.

- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - The platform should offer a diverse range of templates.
  - The platform should allow for easy preview of templates.
  - The platform should allow for easy selection of templates.
  - The platform should allow for easy customization of templates.
  - The platform should allow for easy saving of templates.
- **Complexity**: S

**As a** non-technical founder,
**I want** to customize the design of my website,
**So that** it reflects my brand identity.

- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - The platform should allow for easy customization of colors.
  - The platform should allow for easy customization of fonts.
  - The platform should allow for easy customization of images.
  - The platform should allow for easy customization of layout.
  - The platform should allow for easy preview of changes.
- **Complexity**: M
