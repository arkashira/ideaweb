## Partner Integration Roadmap for IdeaWeb

### 1. **Zapier**
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to automate workflows by connecting IdeaWeb with over 3,000 apps, solving the job of streamlining repetitive tasks and enhancing productivity.
   - **Rationale**: Zapier has a strong affiliate program, allowing us to earn revenue through referrals while providing users with powerful automation capabilities.

### 2. **Stripe**
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier, but no monthly fees; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Large
   - **Value-add**: Facilitates easy payment processing for users who want to monetize their websites, addressing the job of setting up e-commerce functionalities seamlessly.
   - **Rationale**: Stripe offers a robust affiliate program, providing potential revenue-sharing opportunities as users integrate payment solutions on their sites.

### 3. **Mailchimp**
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 500 subscribers and 1,000 emails/month
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Allows users to manage email marketing campaigns directly from their websites, solving the job of engaging with customers effectively.
   - **Rationale**: Mailchimp has an affiliate program that can generate revenue for IdeaWeb while enhancing user engagement capabilities.

### 4. **Airtable**
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1,200 records per base, 2 GB attachment space
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides users with a flexible database solution to manage content and data for their websites, addressing the job of organizing information efficiently.
   - **Rationale**: Airtable has a referral program that can yield revenue for IdeaWeb while offering users a powerful tool for content management.

### 5. **Google Analytics**
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited tracking for up to 10 million hits/month
   - **Integration effort**: Small
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to track website performance and user behavior, solving the job of data-driven decision-making for website improvements.
   - **Rationale**: While Google Analytics does not have a direct affiliate program, its integration is essential for user insights, enhancing the overall value of IdeaWeb.

### 6. **Canva**
   - **Free-tier limits**: Access to basic features; limited templates and storage
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Allows users to create custom graphics and designs for their websites, addressing the job of enhancing visual appeal without needing design skills.
   - **Rationale**: Canva has an affiliate program that can provide additional revenue opportunities while enhancing the design capabilities of IdeaWeb users.

### 7. **Typeform**
   - **Free-tier limits**: 3 forms, 10 questions per form, 100 responses/month
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to create interactive forms and surveys, solving the job of collecting user feedback and data effectively.
   - **Rationale**: Typeform offers an affiliate program, allowing for potential revenue generation while providing users with valuable tools for engagement.

### 8. **Social Media Platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)**
   - **Free-tier limits**: N/A (free to use)
   - **Integration effort**: Small
   - **Value-add**: Facilitates easy sharing and integration of social media feeds, solving the job of increasing website visibility and engagement.
   - **Rationale**: While these platforms do not have direct affiliate programs, their integration is crucial for users looking to enhance their online presence, making IdeaWeb more attractive. 

---

This roadmap outlines strategic partnerships that enhance IdeaWeb's value proposition while providing opportunities for revenue generation through affiliate programs. Each integration addresses specific user jobs, ensuring that non-technical founders can launch and manage their websites effectively.
